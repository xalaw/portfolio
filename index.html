    About Me

    I love collaborating with innovative minds and sparking ideas to formulate solutions down to the very last pixel. This allows me to make a difference in how people experience technology. I'm a highly creative person who can also code. In a team environment, I always give everyone a listening ear so every idea has a chance to prove itself.

    I've been making websites since I was 15. And when I turned 18, I started doing graphic design and majored in Multimedia Design Technology as a Bachelor of Arts. My first job after college involved graphic design, print work and the role of webmaster for a staging / event company called Brown United. The opportunity there allowed me to work daily with SEO optimizations to push their company website to the top of search results, and rebuilding their website from ground up.

    From there, I developed NBC.com's first iPhone mobile web. I collaborated with stakeholders to make their vision come alive from wireframes to code. In 2009, I joined TMZ to transition them through two infrastructure migrations to accommodate for multi-platform content delivery and high traffic volumes, and yearly front-end redesigns on tmz and its sister sites. I also developed in Objective C in XCode for the TMZ mobile app.

    In the past year, I was the Art Director and Webmaster for Katie B Cosmetics. I implemented branding strategies on their website and social media. Later in the year, I freelanced work and also worked as a production manager for a bi-monthly night of inspiring artist in West Hollywood, CA. I also took a course in User Experience and Fullstack Web Development.

    Outside of coding, I am a classically trained keyboard player from the age of 7, and in the past 10 years, I utilized virtual instruments from Native Instruments and Ableton Live to produce and create live instrumentals for a rotating band. I also love photography, makeup application and photo editing. Besides the creative, I love performance cars and collect selective diecast vehicles.
