    About Me

    I love collaborating with innovative minds and sparking ideas to formulate solutions down to the very last pixel. This allows me to make a difference in how people experience technology. I'm a highly creative person that can also code. In a team environment, I always give everyone a listening ear so every idea has a chance to prove itself.

    I've been making websites since I was 15. And when I turned 18, I started doing graphic design and majored in Multimedia Design Technology as a Bachelor of Arts. My first job after college involved graphic and print work and the webmaster for a staging / event company called Brown United.

    From there, I developed NBC.com's first iPhone mobile web. I collaborated with stakeholders to make their vision come alive from wireframes to code. In 2009, I joined TMZ to transition them through two infrastructure migrations to accommodate for multi-platform content delivery, yearly front-end redesigns on tmz and its sister sites. I also developed in Objective C in XCode for TMZ mobile app.

    In the past year, I was the Art Director and Webmaster for Katie B Cosmetics. I implemented branding strategies on their website and social media. Later in the year, I freelanced work and also worked as a production manager for a bi-monthly night of inspiring artist in West Hollywood, CA. I also took courses in User Experience and also a Fullstack Web Development Course.

    Outside of coding, I am a classically trained keyboard player from the age of 7, and in the past 10 years, I have integrated computer software to help assist me in producing and live instrumentalist. I use Ableton Live and Native Instruments' Komplete 11 Ultimate to fill the room with beautiful sounds. I also love photography, makeup application and photoediting. Besides the creative, I love performance cars and collect selective diecast vehicles.
